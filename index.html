About

Dry/Wet is a duo of electronic music in Thessaloniki comprised of George Hantas (born in 1990) and Nikos Vlagoidis (born in 1988) both coming from Serres, Greece. Their passion for electronic music began at a very young age as they had already, each separately, been mixing and playing music, while they were still amateurs at the age of 16, mostly progressive-house sounds. Their paths crossed in Thessaloniki 2007 when George Hantas decided to move there in order to study Music Technology, while Nikos Vlagoidis was already there for studies after his graduation from Music school of Serres. They quickly realised that they shared the same views and ideas around music so, they decided to create the project Dry/Wet and start as a duo. Thereafter, several appearances followed both in Serres and Thessaloniki where they played music, mostly back to back. In 2009 they have begun to experiment in music production and they have been producing their own tracks ever since. They have several releases on respected labels like Monique Speciale Monique Chronique . Their win on Cu on Stage Reworks edition on September of 2015 can not be ignored as they warm up the stage for Ellen Allien and Pan Pot. Their sound is mostly based on dance kick drums and groovy basslines, mixed with techno elements.
